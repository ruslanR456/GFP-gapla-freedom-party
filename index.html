Freedom Party Government Unveils Landmark "Child Protection Act"

Saturday, June 13, 2026

The governing Freedom Party has officially introduced its flagship "Child Protection Act" today, marking what ministers call a fundamental shift in national safety standards for minors.

The legislation, presented to the legislature this morning, serves as a cornerstone of the new Parental Freedom Act of the Federal States of Gapla. This overarching legislative framework is designed to provide protection to minors globally, provided they hold e-residency within Gapla.

A critical component of this initiative is Article 1, which stipulates that all minors over the age of 13 are granted the right to travel to Gapla to seek refuge from their parents in cases of harm—whether mental, physical, or spiritual.
